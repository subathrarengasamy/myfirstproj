What is Funnis?

It's a great way of introducing sport in a very young age.Funnis Academy offers a safe indoor environment.Tennis is the perfect sport for young children to learn basic skills including running,jumping,throwing and catching.

Who all can Participate?

Children who are all above 4 years to under 10 years.

Benefits of Playing Funnis.

Children get awareness of having fun away from screen times. Funnis provides all the sensory experiences where a child need for their development.It teaches physical,literacy,motor planning,eye contact and hand coordination,object to object coordination,analytical thinking and many more.

Session involves:

Six or Eight Sessions.
